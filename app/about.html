About Me

Before starting university I had never looked at a piece of code, I remember sitting down in my first Web Programming lesson in year 1 and thinking how am I ever going to get this!

I quickly realised that it actually made sense and really enjoyed it coming out with 80% overall in PHP. I then progressed onto the 2nd Year where I started to become more aware that I quite enjoyed all web units and they were actually my favourites and wished I could do more of them! I decided to be organised in order to get a good understanding of some basic JavaScript for my 2nd year and purchased the John Duckett book, this allowed me to get 89% in my first JavaScript test then 74% on 3 set tasks.

I also studied Digital Project Management in my 2nd Year and I was a PM for a small group of 5. I really enjoyed this role and it gave me a chance to look closer at how other people chose to learn and I feel we all worked really well as a group, our group project got 77%. It also gave me an insight into an Agile way of working and allowed me to gain knowledge that I am now using to do my final year project.

Outside University

Outside of university my main hobby is horse riding. I have owned horses since I was 3 and they are a big part of my life. I compete nationally in BSJA (British Showjumping) with my current horse Alfie.

I am also a big music fan and regually attend concerts to see a range of different artists.
