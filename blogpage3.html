Performance and Optimisation

Making your site successful entails many different things, once it’s live and out for the world to see you have to be sure that it can perform with the rest. There are a number of different methods you can use to do this.

Code Validation

Code validation might be the most important and one of the simplest to do. The easiest way to check your HTML is to head over to the W3C Markup Validation Service. This allows you to either enter your website URL if your site is live but also gives you the option to upload files that you are working on so you can check as you go along. There is also the option to directly input HTML code to validate.

Page Optimisation

Making sure that your page is fully optimised is a really important part of making your site successful. In an article written by Team Treehouse it goes on to explain why optimising your site for mobile is so important;

Today, 1.2 billion people are accessing the web from mobile devices. An incredible 80% of all internet users use a smartphone. In other words, if they’re online, they are most likely on their phones.

There is also another tool that’s available from Google called Mobile-Friendly Test . This allows you to input your URL and then feeds back if their Googlebots see your site as mobile friendly.

You can also test the speed of your page by using Googles PageSpeed Insights test. This allows you to see how fast your site loads on both mobile and desktop and gives you a mark out of 100 along with feedback so you can improve your site. Then it gives you a mark out of 100 for user experience and shows you the rules your website has passed.

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) Intergration

Once you’ve created your fast, mobile friendly site that validates you will want to make sure that it ranks high in search engine results. This is where SEO comes in; it gives you the chance to make sure that your site is there when people search. The simplest way to start doing some SEO is by including Meta Description in your head section and also keywords. The article by search engine watch lists 22 essentials for optimizing your site, one of which is;

Title tags are used to tell search engines and visitors what your site is about in the most concise and accurate way possible.

Overall

All of these aspects are highly crucial to having a successful site, I have only addressed the basics in this blog post but I will link some more information below if you wish to read more into any of things mentioned above.
